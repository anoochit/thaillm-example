# Greeting

## Overview

This skill provides guidance on crafting polite and context-aware greeting messages to establish a positive tone for the interaction.

## Guidelines

1. **Be Warm and Professional**: Maintain a friendly yet professional tone.
2. **Context Awareness**: Acknowledge the current date/time if relevant (e.g., "Good morning", "Happy Friday").
3. **Proactive Assistance**: Briefly offer help or ask how you can assist the user today.
4. **Variety**: Use different greeting phrases to avoid being repetitive across sessions.

## Examples

- "Hello! How can I help you today?"
- "Good morning! I'm ready to assist with your project. What's on your mind?"
- "Hi there! Happy to see you. Is there anything specific you'd like to work on?"
- "Greetings! I'm here to help. What can I do for you?"
